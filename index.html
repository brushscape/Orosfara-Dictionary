Suffixes Denote Parts of Speach

Ending Part of Speech
-a noun
-o verb
-n object
-i adjective
-e adverb
-u command

Example Sentences

The fish likes clean water.
Peisa jemo safi nitan.

Quickly, catch the bug!
Fekame juaju kitan!

The rabbit wants to see a bird.
Korkosa kafar-fero parinan.

I walk to the store.
Yenapa katito ir pantara.

Common Particles

Particle Usage
um indicate a dependent clause
tik indicate the time-frame and/or tense of the verb
ki indicate a statement is a question
ha indicate a statement is an exclamation (optional)

Example Sentences

Did I see you yesterday?
Perten tik fero jenapan ki?

Come here right now!
Simsim tik janu yejak ha!

I can't remember what I was doing.
Nakap-memoro um seyo kiyan tik atit.

My friend heard you can eat and sleep simultaneously.
Atit tik tosa as yenapa finano um kap-heko an yanamo turan.
